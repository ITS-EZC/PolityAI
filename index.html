Why PolityAI

Clarity you can check, not spin you have to trust.

Plenty of tools will tell you what to think about a bill. We tell you what the bill says — and let you decide.

Built on the record

Every explanation traces back to the actual bill text and the roll-call vote. No paraphrase you can't verify, no editorializing.

Plain English, full substance

We translate statute into language anyone can follow — without dropping the detail that actually changes how the law works.

Nonpartisan by design

We surface what a bill does and how each side voted — not a verdict on whether it's good. The judgment stays with you.

Always current

We track legislation as it moves through committee, the floor, and amendment — so you're never reading a summary that's gone stale.

How it works

From statute to clarity, in four steps.

A pipeline that keeps every plain-English answer anchored to its source.

01

Ingest

We pull bills, amendments, and roll-call votes straight from official legislative sources as they're published.

02

Translate

Our models convert dense statutory language into clear, plain-English explanations a first-time reader can follow.

03

Verify

Each explanation is anchored to the underlying text, so every claim is checkable against the source — not invented.

04

Surface

You get the what, the who, and the how-they-voted — searchable, shareable, and ready to act on.
