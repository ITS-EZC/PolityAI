How it works

From statute to clarity, in four steps.

A pipeline that keeps every plain-English answer anchored to its source.

01

Ingest

We pull bills, amendments, and roll-call votes straight from official legislative sources as they're published.

02

Translate

Our models convert dense statutory language into clear, plain-English explanations a first-time reader can follow.

03

Verify

Each explanation is anchored to the underlying text, so every claim is checkable against the source — not invented.

04

Surface

You get the what, the who, and the how-they-voted — searchable, shareable, and ready to act on.
